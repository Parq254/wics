Our Mission

"To foster a more open and accountable government by providing citizens with clear, comprehensive, and accessible information about government operations, policies, and expenditures."

Who We Are

wics.gov is a non-partisan, independent organization dedicated to increasing government transparency and accountability. Established in 2015, we work tirelessly to gather, analyze, and present government data in a way that is easily understandable and actionable for all citizens.

What We Do

  • Collect and organize publicly available government data
  • Create user-friendly tools for exploring government operations and expenditures
  • Provide analysis and context to help citizens understand complex government processes
  • Advocate for increased government transparency and open data initiatives
  • Collaborate with journalists, researchers, and policymakers to promote informed public discourse

Our Funding

wics.gov is funded through a combination of foundation grants, individual donations, and revenue from our data services. We do not accept government funding to maintain our independence. For a detailed breakdown of our funding sources and expenditures, please visit our Finance page.
